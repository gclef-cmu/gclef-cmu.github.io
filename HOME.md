# CMU Generative Creativity Lab

<!-- <img src="./static/logos/gclef.png" width="200px"> -->

We are the Generative Creativity Lab (G-CLef), part of the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University. Our mission is to **empower and enrich human creativity and productivity with generative AI**. We focus primarily on the intersection of music and AI, though we also work on other applications such as programming, gaming, and natural language.

The Generative Creativity Lab is led by <a class="link-plain" href="https://chrisdonahue.com">Chris Donahue</a>, Dannenberg Assistant Professor at <a class="link-plain" href="https://csd.cmu.edu/">CMU CSD</a>.
