# Pure Vibes

Pure Vibes is an extension to Pure Data allowing for AI agent control.

You can [download Pure Vibes here](https://github.com/gclef-cmu/pure-vibes/releases).

Please leave feedback on [this Google form](https://forms.gle/TRDoD46YURA8N41Z8). 

If you prefer, you may also open up a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/gclef-cmu/pure-vibes/issues).

A mailing list is coming soon.

**Privacy statement**. The developers of Pure Vibes do not collect any of your usage data. Please leave all feedback for the Pure Vibes devs on the form above. Many AI agent providers (e.g. Anthropic, OpenAI) _will_ collect your usage data, subject to their own terms of service.
